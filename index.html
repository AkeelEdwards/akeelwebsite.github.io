What size blocks do you deal with?

For us, it’s not just about the size of the block, it’s helping leaseholders with managing their homes, get in touch for a chat about what we can do for you.

Are you regulated?

Yes, we are associate members of ARMA, regulated by HMRC and we have Client Money Protection to keep your funds safe. In addition, we’re also registered with the Information Commissions Office and are members of the Property Ombudsman.

Are you insured?

Yes, we have several vital insurances. including a Professional Indemnity policy in place for £2,000,000.

Is moving agents painful?

We have vast experience in helping leaseholders move their management over to us, we have a tight process with all the checks and balances. Keeping you informed during the process is also key to make sure you are comfortable along the journey and the move is as pain-free as possible.

How about fire safety and cladding?

In recent years, fire safety, particularly in residential buildings, has become an integral part of the duties of a managing agent and one which we can see expanding in the coming years. We have experience dealing with External Wall Reviews at over 20 developments. Many have a resolution specified, engineered, subsequently issued for tender and completed. We also have experience making several successful applications to the building safety fund.

There will be a single point of contact who is always happy to discuss any concerns over the phone or in person.

What’s your minimum contract term?

To keep things simple, our minimum contract period is 364 days.
